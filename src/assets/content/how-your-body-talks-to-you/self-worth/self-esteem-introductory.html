Self-Esteem Introductory

Self-Esteem has been found to be the most powerful predictor of happiness.

Positive self-esteem is one of the most important elements in good mental/emotional health.

Positive self worth increases confidence and success in school: academic success and reading ability.

Positive self-esteem also leads to better job satisfaction later in life.

Negative feelings of self-worth create self defeating behaviors ie risky sex, substance abuse, that lead to depressive feelings.

Low self-worth can lead to social problems, aggressive behavior, repetitive relationship problems.

Poor self-esteem can lead to mood disorders, eating disorders a well as physical disease.

Discover ways in which low self esteem can be remedied.
