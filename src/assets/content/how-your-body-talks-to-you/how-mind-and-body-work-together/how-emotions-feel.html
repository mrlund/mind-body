How Emotions Feel

Where different emotions are experienced in the body

Activity 1

Think of something in the last couple of days that was upsetting - try to feel exactly how you felt - where do you feel that physically in your body? Every time you experience emotion - your body gets into a pattern - if you don’t feel it and release it - that’s how disease is experienced in the body.

The repetitive pattern of your thinking can lead to either disease or wellbeing and you can train your thoughts just like you train your body.

Activity 2

Positive emotions can affect your health. So let us practice prioritizing positive emotions.

Break up in pairs – each partner will tell a story about something positive. Try to really feel the positive emotion and let go of negative thoughts.
