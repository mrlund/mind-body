How Emotions Feel

Different emotions displayed as their percieved body part

Complete the following activities

Think of something in the last couple of days that was upsetting - feel how you felt - where do you feel that in your body? Every time you feel that kind of feeling - your body gets into a pattern - if you don’t feel it and release it - that’s how disease is experienced in the body

The repetitive pattern of your thinking produces disease or wellbeing and you can train your thoughts just like you train your body.
