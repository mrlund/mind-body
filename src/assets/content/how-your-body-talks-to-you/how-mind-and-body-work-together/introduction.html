A Pain In The Neck

Discuss in a group:

  1. What are all the emotions Zinab is feeling?
  2. What does Jenn mean when she says that Zinab is "carryign around too much stuff"?
  3. How do Jenn and Zinab demonstrate how exercise and sleeping affect stress?
Through Jenn and Zinab, we can see that emotions and our body are tightly linked. Taking care of one means taking care of the other!

Discuss with a partner:

Let's let out some stress like Zinab! Keeping negative emotions in your head can hurt you. It's important to vent something. Turn to the person sitting next to you and talk about one thing that has been causing you stress recently.
