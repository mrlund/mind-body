I'm Feeling So...

How different emotions are expressed nonverbally

In this activity, the group will practice nonverbal communication, sometimes called “body language”

  • Each of you will have a turn to demonstrate the feeling on your card while saying this phrase out loud.
  • You may use sounds, facial expressions, and body language, but you may not say anything other than the words written on this newsprint.
  • The group will then try to guess what emotion you are displaying.
  • Keep in mind that some emotions are more difficult to express than others. Just do your best to act out the emotion you get.
  • If no one in the group guesses your emotion, you will have one chance to perform it again. If the group still can’t guess the emotion, show them your card.
